About me

Hello, I'm Churchill.

I'm a mechanical engineer who enjoys the entire arc of building physical things — from the first conceptual design, through simulation and analysis, all the way to a printed part you can hold in your hand.

My work usually sits at the intersection of design, simulation, and code. I like problems where a clear idea has to survive contact with material properties, manufacturing constraints, and real loading conditions. I try to keep my work clean, well-documented, and easy for the next engineer to pick up.

When I'm not at a workstation, I'm usually tuning a 3D printer, brainstorming design solution and validation, or reading about how things actually break.
